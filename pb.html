Case Studies

EduAdvance: Automated System for Career Advancements of Faculty in Higher Education

Role: Team Leader
Event: Smart India Hackathon 2024

As the team leader for the Smart India Hackathon 2024, I spearheaded a project titled EduAdvance, which focused on developing an automated system aimed at enhancing career advancement opportunities for faculty members in higher education.

Proposed Solution:

  • Performance Tracking: Allows faculty to monitor their achievements and areas for improvement.
  • Mentorship Opportunities: Connects faculty members with mentors to guide their career progression.
  • Professional Development Resources: Offers access to training and workshops tailored to individual needs.
  • Administrative Automation: Streamlines administrative tasks to allow faculty to focus more on teaching and research.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Career Guidance: Utilizing AI and machine learning to provide tailored career recommendations.
  • Gamification Elements: Incorporating badges and challenges to increase engagement and motivation.
